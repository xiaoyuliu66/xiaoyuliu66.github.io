Biography

I am currently a Data & Applied Scientist at Microsoft. I have graduated as a PhD at Southern Methodist University Department of Computer Science. I was advised by Prof. LiGuo Huang. I also worked with collegues from Human Language Technology Research Institute in UTD to apply machine learning on software engineering.

I am interested in some research topics such as API recommendation, software licensing, software change impact analysis, and software change intent analysis. Details can be found in my publications.
